Thank you for choosing Mindful Symbols. This Privacy Policy describes how I, rainCheng (an individual developer), handle your information.

My philosophy is simple: I believe privacy is a fundamental right. Since Mindful Symbols runs entirely on your device, I do not collect, store, or have access to your personal data or conversation history.

1. No Server & On-Device Processing

Mindful Symbols is different from many other AI apps. It utilizes a proprietary AI model that runs 100% locally on your iPhone.

  • No Data Transmission: When you chat with Mindful Symbols, your input and the AI's output are processed directly by your device's processor. Your conversations never leave your device.
  • No Cloud Storage: I do not operate a server to store your messages. Therefore, it is technically impossible for me to read, share, or sell your conversations.

2. Information Collection

Since the App does not require user registration or login, I do not collect any personal information (such as your name, phone number, or email address).

The only data handling involved is strictly for the App's functionality:

  • Subscriptions (StoreKit 2): Transactions are handled securely by Apple via the App Store. I do not have access to your credit card or billing information. The App only checks the receipt locally on your device to determine if you have a valid "VIP" status.
  • Local Trial Status (Keychain): To provide a free trial opportunity, the App saves a secure, anonymous flag in your device's Keychain. This is strictly to verify if the device has already used the free trial. This data remains on your device and is not uploaded anywhere.

3. Third-Party Services & Analytics

I do not use third-party analytics services (such as Google Firebase, Mixpanel, or Facebook Analytics) that track your behavior or link usage data to your identity.

Any crash reports or diagnostic data collected by Apple (if you have opted-in to share diagnostics with developers in your iOS settings) are completely anonymous and aggregated. I use this anonymous data solely to fix bugs and improve stability.

4. Data Sharing

Because I do not collect your data, I have no data to share with any third parties, advertisers, or marketing agencies.
